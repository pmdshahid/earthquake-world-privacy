Privacy Policy - Earthquake World

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Earthquake World ("the App") is developed by HaSha. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Access

Location Data

The App accesses your device's location only when you explicitly enable "Smart Alerts" in Settings. This location is used one time to set a center point for filtering nearby earthquake alerts. Location data is stored only on your device (locally) and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can disable this feature at any time in the App's settings.

Notification & Background Monitoring

The App runs a background service to check for new earthquakes periodically, even when the App is closed. This is used solely to deliver earthquake alert notifications. No personal data is collected or transmitted during this process.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

2. Third-Party Data Sources

The App fetches public earthquake data from two independent scientific agencies:

  • United States Geological Survey (USGS) - earthquake.usgs.gov
  • European-Mediterranean Seismological Centre (EMSC) - seismicportal.eu

These are read-only public data feeds. No personal information is sent to these services.

3. Permissions Used

  • Internet: To fetch live earthquake data.
  • Location (optional): Only for the "Smart Alerts" feature, described above.
  • Notifications: To alert you about new earthquakes.
  • Foreground Service: To keep monitoring for earthquakes while the App runs in the background.
  • Battery Optimization Exemption (optional): To improve reliability of background alerts. This is optional and requested only if you choose to enable it in Settings.

4. Data Storage

All settings and preferences (such as your chosen region, magnitude filter, and alert preferences) are stored locally on your device using standard Android app storage. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is a general information tool suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
